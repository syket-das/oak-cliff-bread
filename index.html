Our Story

A Family Bakery Rooted in Texas Grain

We're Tyler and Chayanne Rooney, and Oak Cliff Bread is our family bakery. We started baking for our neighbors in 2020, dropping off loaves on porches across Oak Cliff. What began as a pandemic passion project grew into a community staple — first at the Dallas Farmers Market, and now at our permanent home inside Tyler Station.

When we started, we made certain commitments: to use slow fermentation and local grain. We partner with Barton Springs Mill to source heirloom Texas wheat, grown and milled just three hours south. Long fermentation increases complexity in flavor and digestibility — it's bread the way it was meant to be made.
